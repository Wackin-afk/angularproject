About

We believe in transparency, cooperation globally, and the free exchange of talent and ideas. A forum for the sharing of these technical and humanitarian discussions is given for meetings, conferences and other events. We are also commited to creating a secure, constructive and supportive atmosphere for all participants in conferences, activites adn meetings who participate in these discussions with us.



Conferences are a part of the legacy of this organization and continue today to be a catalyst for creativity. We are committed to developing strategies that support and expand the conference activites throught the world.
